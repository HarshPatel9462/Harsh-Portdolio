Perfumier by Josh

Perfumier is an online encyclopedia of perfumes, a perfume magazine and a community of perfume lovers. Perfumier informs their readers about new perfume launches, about famous fragrances and less-known but wonderful scents. Together we travel in time and space, where perfumes are the shining stars we use to navigate. We learn about their history, we discover far-away places and respectfully explore the life we see around us, always taking time to be amazed by Nature. Perfumier is a place to learn from each other and relax in the company of your soul mates.

Perfumier is an independent magazine, based in San Diego (California, USA). Perfumier is available in several different languages and is open to everybody. You are welcome to contribute your reviews, read our articles and other content for your individual information, and to take part in our forum discussions as well. We only ask you to be considerate of each other to enable everyone to enjoy their experience.

Press Relations

If you are a perfume brand, no matter big or small, we want to hear news from you! We prefer email contact (feel free to email editor@Perfumier.com or any of the team members directly). The more comprehensive your material is, the easier it will be for our editors to include your creations into our encyclopedia or announce them in the News section.

Ayush Binji

Founder

Ayush was born in Delhi, studied several languages and literature in UK, and lived in london and canada, where the idea of perfumier was born. Now he resides in Toronto.

Harsh Patel

Co-founder& CTO

Harsh is the co-founder of perfumier with Ayush. He studied and later taught mechanical engineering at Waterloo University. He has a software development work background and vast dot-com web technology experience. Harsh's primary focus is on the development and maintenance of the technology of perfumier but also is responsible for things like finance, marketing, and sales.
